About Me

I've always been in the business of creating something from nothing. Whether that means:

  • Composing original content for social media marketing
  • Designing a sales analysis report using advanced Excel features
  • Conceiving and organizing the first Autism Awareness Week at Brandeis University
  • Recruiting and managing a team of writers and editors creating web content for SEO
  • Teaching myself how to play piano and write songs
  • Developing an award-winning comedy sketch
  • Turning a crafting hobby into a side-business
  • Etc., Etc.

While my personal interests and professional experience are eclectic, they all have one thing in common: creativity.

I create products, experiences, processes -- and I know how to share these creations through clear communication and savvy strategy.

I am looking forward to gaining a whole slew of new skills at SIB, and most importantly, I'm excited to meet the movers and shakers of the Boston startup scene. I can't wait to see what you're creating.
